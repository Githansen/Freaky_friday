In our hotel, you can choose between 3 rooms. You can check out any time you like, and our reception is open 24/7. From your room, you can order food from the hotel restaurant, and for a fee we can buy you groceries. Feel free to ask our receptionists if you have any questions. Our rooms are affordable and quite simple, as we know most of our visitors use our hotel to sleep in, and spend as much time as possible sightseeing.

The Jumbo

Info about The Jumbo

  • Bedrooms: 1

  • Persons: 1

  • Bathrooms: 1

  • WI-FI: Yes

  • TV: No

Room info

As you can see from the pictures, this is a simple room with only a bedroom and a bathroom. It's our cheapest room, but the standard is still high. From this room, you can see Big Ben. Our customers appreciate the simplicity of our room. London is a big city, and you usually don't have time to sit in your room if you want to explore the city efficiently.

Bathroom

The bathroom is unfortunately not optimal for wheelchair users, and you might need assistance in order to move around. If that's the case, we will give you an upgrade for the same price. Please leave a comment when you book this room so we can do fix this for you.
